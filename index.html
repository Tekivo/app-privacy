Privacy Policy for Universal Remote (TV)

Effective Date: August 15, 2025

Your privacy is important to us. Our Universal Remote (TV) app is designed to be simple and secure. This policy explains the limited information our app accesses to function.

Information We Access

We believe in collecting as little data as possible. Our app does not collect any personal information such as your name, email, photos, or contacts.

The only information the app accesses is:

  • Local Network Information: To work as a remote control, the app must scan your local Wi-Fi network. This is done for the sole purpose of discovering compatible Smart TVs (e.g., Samsung, LG, Roku) that you can control. This process may detect the local IP addresses and names of devices on your network.

How We Use Information

The network information discovered by the app is used only on your device to establish a connection with your TV.

  • This information is not stored, shared, or transmitted to our servers or any third party.
  • For your convenience, the app may save the connection details of your TV (like its local IP address) securely on your device to allow for quicker reconnection in the future. This data never leaves your device.

Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the updated version available within the app.
